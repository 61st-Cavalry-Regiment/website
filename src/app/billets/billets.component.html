Army Infantry Orion Company

Our Infantry detachment is our main force in the field. If you are looking for the main combative positions in the field, this is where you will want to be. Infantry will be utilizing both Humvees as well as select MRAPS and the Strider as necessary. If you are interested in seeing some pictures from operations, click on the Picture to the left and you will see our Infantry in action. More information on the qualification an Infantryman may obtain will also be included.

Army Air Phoenix

Our Army Aviation is the main supporting element in the 61st utilizing Chinooks, Black Hawks, and Apaches to complete missions and support the Infantry movement. If you are looking to play a more supportive role, then look no further than our Army Aviation.

Army Medical Corps Perseus

The medic is everyone's best friend. This is a little different from having a medical qualification as there is being a qualed medic and being a slotted medic. A medics main job is to make sure the squad is at the Tip top combat effectiveness. The medic will also determine with a MEDEVAC is necessary.
